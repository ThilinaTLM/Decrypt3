Ideathon

Do you always have brilliant ideas, but can never find a place to get people interested? Then the Ideathon is the perfect platform for you. Pitch your ideas to some of the most brilliant minds in Sri Lanka and win cash prizes. Don't wait up!!!

First make a group of 4 people. Choose a leader and nice name for your team. You can register decrypt-ideathon by filling the form which is given below. There are set of few rules and information that you need to know which you can download it from link below. Finally you need to submit a Project Proposal for your idea.
